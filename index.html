CHAMPS Classroom Expectations

Conversation

No talking. Remain completely silent.
Whisper only to your immediate neighbor.
Quiet conversation allowed with a small group.
Regular conversation is permitted.

Help

First, check your notes or instructions for answers.
If you can't find it, quietly ask a partner for help.
If you still need help, raise your hand for the teacher.

Activity

Work by yourself, no collaboration.
Work with one partner.
Work with a small group.
Whole class activity.

Movement

Stay seated at your desk.
Get up only for specific reasons (e.g., supplies).
Move freely as needed.

Participation

Select all types of participation expected.

Chromebooks

Do not use Chromebooks during this activity.
Share Chromebooks with a partner/group.
Limited Chromebook use; music is allowed.
Limited Chromebook use; music is not allowed.
